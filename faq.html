What is PawCare?

üêï Services & Care

What happens during a pet sitting visit?

+
During each visit, we'll feed your pet, provide fresh water, give medications if needed, clean litter boxes, provide playtime and exercise, and send you photo updates. We'll also bring in mail and water plants if requested.

Do you walk multiple dogs at once?

+
We offer both individual and small group walks. Group walks are limited to 3 dogs maximum to ensure safety and individual attention. Let us know your preference when booking.

Can you administer medication to my pet?

+
Yes! Our pet sitters are trained to administer oral medications, topical treatments, and injections (like insulin). Please provide detailed instructions and ensure medications are clearly labeled.

What types of pets do you care for?

+
We care for dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, and most other small pets. If you have an exotic pet, please contact us to discuss their specific care needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Payment is typically due at the time of service or can be set up for automatic billing with recurring services.

Do you offer discounts for multiple pets?

+
Yes! We offer a 10% discount for each additional pet in the same household. Package deals also provide significant savings for regular services.

Are there any additional fees?

+
Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. Additional charges may apply for holiday bookings, last-minute requests (less than 24 hours), or services outside our normal coverage area.

What happens in case of a pet emergency?

+
We have 24/7 emergency protocols in place. If your pet needs immediate veterinary care, we'll contact you immediately and take your pet to your preferred vet or the nearest emergency clinic. We always have your emergency contact information on hand.

How do you ensure my home's security?

+
All our staff are background-checked and bonded. We use secure key management systems, lock all doors and windows, and can adjust lights and blinds to maintain a lived-in appearance. We never share your address or schedule with anyone.

Do I need to provide vaccination records?

+
For boarding and group dog walking services, we require proof of current vaccinations (rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella). This protects all pets in our care. Individual services don't require vaccination records.
